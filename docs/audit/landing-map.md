### FAQ Section (Top 10)

1. **Is this NCAA-compliant?**
   - Yes, our platform is built with compliance officers in mind. Every deal generates audit trails, and schools can customize rules. We're designed to meet current NCAA guidelines and adapt as rules evolve.

2. **How do payments work?**
   - Escrow holds funds until deliverables are verified (e.g., Instagram post goes live). Athletes get paid via Stripe ACH within 2-3 business days. Payouts are tracked with receipts.

3. **What if there's a dispute?**
   - Our dispute resolution flow keeps all receipts, screenshots, and evidence in one place. Schools can mediate, or parties can use the audit trail for third-party resolution.

4. **Do athletes need training?**
   - Yes. Athletes must complete NIL 101 (free, 30 minutes) before creating their first deal. Schools can add custom training requirements.

5. **Can parents see deals?**
   - Yes. Athletes can grant "Family Vault" access to parents, giving them read-only visibility into all contracts and payments.

6. **What's included in the compliance dashboard?**
   - Real-time deal tracking, disclosure compliance, NCAA export (PDF), rule violation alerts, and cap spend monitoring for schools.

7. **How do disclosures work?**
   - Our Disclosure Pack Builder generates FTC-compliant captions (with #ad tags) for Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. Athletes copy/paste and track proof-of-posting.

8. **Is there a mobile app?**
   - We have a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on iOS and Android. Install it to your home screen for push notifications and on-the-go approvals. Native apps coming Q2 2026.

9. **How much does it cost?**
   - Athletes and brands: Free to browse. Transaction fees apply to deals. Schools: Custom pricing based on roster size. [Link to pricing page]

10. **What integrations are supported?**
    - Plaid (banking), Stripe (payments), DocuSign (e-signatures), Resend (email), Twilio (SMS). Coming soon: Instagram, Twitter, TikTok for automatic proof-of-posting.

## Copy Style Guide

### Tone
- **Professional but approachable** (not stuffy)
- **Compliance-first, not compliance-obsessed** (avoid fear-mongering)
- **Empowering** (you're in control)
- **Transparent** (no hidden fees, no surprises)

### Voice
- **Active voice** ("You create deals" not "Deals are created")
- **Short sentences** (avg 15 words)
- **Bullet points over paragraphs**
- **Numbers over adjectives** ("[X] schools" not "Many schools")

### Forbidden Words
- ‚ùå "Revolutionary" (overused)
- ‚ùå "Game-changer" (clich√©)
